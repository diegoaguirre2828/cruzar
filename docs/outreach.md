## Dispatcher demo script (for the 30-min setup call)

When a dispatcher replies and books a call, run this 30-min flow:

1. **First 5 min — listen.** "Tell me about your fleet — how many trucks, which bridges, biggest pain right now."
2. **Next 5 min — show the calculator.** Open /for-fleets with their numbers loaded into the slider. Say the dollar amount out loud.
3. **Next 10 min — set up their account live.** Sign up → add their fleet name → add 1-2 driver names with phones → show the magic-link they get → walk through the dispatcher dashboard.
4. **Next 5 min — alerts.** Set up alerts for their 2-3 most-used bridges. Show what the push notification looks like (use the test endpoint we built).
5. **Last 5 min — close.** "First 30 days free, no card now. After that it's $49.99/mo. If it's not saving you 30 min of truck time per month I refund your first month — no questions. Sound fair?"

If they hesitate: "Want me to bill you in 30 days, or just give it 30 days and we talk again?"
